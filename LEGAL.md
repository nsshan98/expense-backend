## 2. Privacy Policy

### 2.1. Information We Collect
*   **Personal Information**: Name, Email Address.
*   **Preferences**: Timezone, Currency Settings.
*   **Financial Data**: Transactions, Budgets, Subscription names, amounts, and dates.
*   **Secrets**: If you use AI features, we may securely store your Google Gemini API Key.

### 2.2. How We Use Your Data
*   **Service Delivery**: To categorize your expenses and calculate budget usage.
*   **Notifications**: To send you emails regarding subscription renewals, payment confirmations, and password resets via our email service providers.
*   **Analytics**: To generate visual dashboards of your spending habits.

### 2.3. Third-Party Services
We share data with specific third-party providers to function:
*   **Google Gemini (AI)**: If enabled, transaction data is sent to Google for analysis/categorization.
*   **Cloudinary**: Used for storing media files (e.g., receipt images or avatars).
*   **SMTP Services**: We use third-party email services (e.g., Gmail/Outlook via Nodemailer) to send system alerts.

### 2.4. Data Security
We implement security measures including:
*   **Encryption**: Passwords are hashed using Argon2/Bcrypt.
*   **Secure API**: All data transmission occurs over secure channels.

## 3. Refund Policy

### 3.1. Manual Payment Processing
Since our system operates on a **Manual Payment Verification** model, payments are verified by human administrators before services are rendered.

### 3.2. Refund Eligibility
*   **Pending Orders**: You may cancel a request before it is verified for a full cancellation.
*   **Verified Subscriptions**: Once a subscription is active, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.
*   **Technical Issues**: If a technical error prevents you from accessing paid premium features, you are eligible for a refund or service extension.

### 3.3. Requesting a Refund
To request a refund:
1.  Contact our support team with your **Transaction ID** and **Email Address**.
2.  Our team will review the transaction against our bank/ledger records.
3.  Approved refunds will be processed manually back to your original payment method. Please allow up to **7-14 business days** for processing.

### 3.4. Non-Refundable Items
*   Service usage periods that have already elapsed.
*   Fees incurred by your own API usage (e.g., Google Gemini bills).
